About Gauntlet Tiers

Gauntlet Tiers is a community-driven ranking system for Minecraft PvP players. We host regular tournaments across multiple game modes including Sword, Mace, Axe, UHC, Pot, Crystal, and SMP PvP.

Our tier system runs from HT1 (the absolute elite) down to LT5 (entry level), giving every player a clear ladder to climb. We believe in transparent, merit-based rankings — if you perform in tournaments, your tier reflects it.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just getting started in competitive PvP, Gauntlet Tiers has a place for you. Join the Discord, get ranked, and start climbing.
